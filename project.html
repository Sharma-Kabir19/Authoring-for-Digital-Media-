The Hunger Games

This project explores and compares the user experience of two food delivery giants — Swiggy and Zomato. From app navigation and visual design to ease of ordering and overall satisfaction, it dives into what each platform gets right (and wrong) in serving their users. It's not just about food, it's about the journey — so who truly delivers the better experience?
